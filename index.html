Asset Tokenisation and its Impact on Financial Markets

A comprehensive predictive analytics study across four tokenised asset classes — Cryptocurrencies, Tokenised Stocks, NFTs, and Virtual Land — comparing statistical, machine learning, and deep learning models to evaluate investment potential and forecast accuracy across 9,350+ daily records.

Asset Classes Compared

Approach: Built end-to-end ML pipelines using ARIMA, Prophet, Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM, and LSTM. Engineered features including RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, rolling volatility, and lagged returns. Developed a weighted investment scoring framework combining ROI, Sharpe Ratio, Max Drawdown, and forecast accuracy. Found LSTM outperformed all models for volatile assets (XRP MAPE reduced from 49% to 5.7%), while enhanced XGBoost excelled on stable equities. Identified that NFTs and virtual land are driven by speculation (weak crypto correlation: 0.11–0.27), while tokenised stocks offered the strongest risk-adjusted returns.
